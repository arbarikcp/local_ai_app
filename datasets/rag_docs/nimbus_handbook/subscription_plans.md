# Subscription Plans

Nimbus offers four plans:

- **Free** — 5GB storage, 2 devices, community support.
- **Personal** — 200GB storage, 5 devices, email support, $6.99/month.
- **Business** — 2TB storage per user, unlimited devices, priority support, mandatory 2FA,
  $12.99/user/month.
- **Enterprise** — custom storage, SSO, dedicated account manager, custom pricing via sales
  contact.

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial; no credit card is required to start a trial on
Personal or Business. Switching between Personal and Business plans is self-service; moving
to or from Enterprise requires contacting sales.
