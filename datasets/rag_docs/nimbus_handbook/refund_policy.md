# Refund Policy

Nimbus offers refunds within 14 days of an initial paid-plan purchase, no questions asked.
Requests after 14 days are evaluated case by case and are not guaranteed. Refunds are not
offered for renewal charges on annual plans if requested after the 14-day window, even if
most of the annual period remains unused.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method and typically appear within 5-10 business
days, depending on your bank or card issuer. To request a refund, contact support with your
account email and the charge date.
