# Account Deletion

To delete your Nimbus account permanently, go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account.
Nimbus requires identity verification before processing a deletion request: you must confirm
via a link sent to your account email, and accounts with 2FA enabled must also enter a valid
2FA code.

Deletion is not immediate — there is a 30-day grace period during which you can cancel the
deletion by signing back in. After 30 days, all files, shared links, and account metadata are
permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Nimbus recommends exporting your data (see
data_export.md) before requesting deletion.
