# Cancelling Your Subscription

To cancel a paid subscription, go to Account Settings > Billing > Subscription and select
"Cancel Plan." Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you keep
paid-plan features until then, and Nimbus does not offer partial-period refunds for
cancellation (see refund_policy.md for the cases that are refundable).

After a paid plan ends, your account automatically reverts to the Free plan. If your stored
data exceeds the Free plan's 5GB limit, the account becomes read-only until you either delete
data or resubscribe; data is never deleted automatically for exceeding a storage limit.
