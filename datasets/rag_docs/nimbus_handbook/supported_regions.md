# Supported Regions

Nimbus stores data in one of three regions, selectable at account creation and changeable
afterward only by contacting support: US (Virginia), EU (Frankfurt), and APAC (Singapore).
Enterprise customers can request data residency guarantees restricting storage to a single
named region for compliance purposes.

Nimbus is available for sign-up worldwide, but customer support response times are fastest
during US business hours (9am-6pm ET) for Free and Personal plans. Business and Enterprise
plans receive follow-the-sun support with no regional restriction.
