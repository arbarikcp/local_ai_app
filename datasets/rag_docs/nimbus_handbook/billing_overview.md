# Billing Overview

Nimbus bills subscriptions monthly or annually, charged automatically on the plan's renewal
date. Annual plans receive a 20% discount compared to paying monthly. All charges are in USD
regardless of your account's region.

Your next billing date and amount are always visible in Account Settings > Billing >
Subscription. Nimbus does not prorate mid-cycle plan changes for downgrades; downgrades take
effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Upgrades take effect immediately and are
charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current cycle.

For details on accepted payment methods, see payment_methods.md. For refund eligibility, see
refund_policy.md.
