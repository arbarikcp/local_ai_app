# Data Export

You can export all of your Nimbus data at any time from Account Settings > Privacy > Export
My Data. Nimbus packages your files and account metadata into a downloadable zip archive;
generation typically takes under an hour for accounts under 100GB, and can take up to 24
hours for larger accounts.

Export download links expire after 7 days. If your account is suspended for a billing issue
(see payment_methods.md), read access and data export both remain available for 30 days after
suspension before any data is scheduled for deletion.
