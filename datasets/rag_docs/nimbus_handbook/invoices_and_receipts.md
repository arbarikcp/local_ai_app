# Invoices and Receipts

Every successful charge generates a receipt, available in Account Settings > Billing >
Invoice History, downloadable as a PDF. Receipts include your billing address, plan name,
billing period, and tax breakdown where applicable.

Nimbus does not email receipts by default; you can enable "Email me a receipt for every
charge" in Billing preferences if you want a copy sent automatically. Historical invoices
older than 24 months are archived and available on request from support, with a turnaround
of up to 5 business days.
