# Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Nimbus supports two-factor authentication using a time-based one-time password (TOTP) app
such as Google Authenticator or Authy. 2FA is optional on the Free and Personal plans, and
mandatory on the Business and Enterprise plans for all workspace members.

To enable 2FA, go to Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication and scan the
displayed QR code with your authenticator app. Nimbus will show you 10 single-use backup
codes; store these somewhere safe, since each can be used once if you lose access to your
authenticator app.

If you lose both your authenticator app and your backup codes, you must go through Nimbus's
manual identity verification process, which can take up to 3 business days.
