# Payment Methods

Nimbus accepts Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit and debit cards, as well as
PayPal. Nimbus does not accept direct bank transfers, cryptocurrency, or gift cards.

You can update your payment method at any time in Account Settings > Billing > Payment
Method. If a scheduled payment fails, Nimbus retries the charge up to 3 times over 7 days
before suspending write access to your account (read access and downloads remain available
for 30 days after suspension, per data_export.md).

Business and Enterprise plans may additionally pay by invoice with net-30 terms; contact
sales to enable invoice billing.
